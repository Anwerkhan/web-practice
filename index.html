Exclusive living trumps in residence The Vliet

Residence De Vliet is a luxury new-build project in the district “Het Schelleken”, an old fishing village right in the historic core of charming Rupelmonde.

Thanks to a contemporary architecture, the landscaping and the accompanying privacy, you can count on living with an exclusive character. The excellent location on the water and the unique view of the Scheldt complete the picture.

Project developer IMWO Invest is fully committed to sustainable construction and living, so that the high quality of living is a certainty. Both today and in the future.

Vision of the architect

Residence De Vliet is a luxury new-build project in the district “Het Schelleken”, an old fishing village right in the historic core of charming Rupelmonde.

In addition to the exceptional location, the contemporary architectural style and well-maintained landscaping give this project a particularly exclusive character. The building has a semi-closed streetscape and is opened up to the south, where large windows and terraces are provided, so that the indoor and outdoor spaces flow into each other. By using light gray facing bricks in combination with black exterior joinery, alternating with the rhythm of the vertical windows in the north facade and large window surfaces in the south facade, this design results in a sleek and airy whole.

Anything but a sin to stay in Rupelmonde

The historic character of Rupelmonde offers you a lot of attention right at your front door.

As soon as you walk out of the residence, you immediately run into the Spanish Mill, which dates from before 1187.

In the castle ruins Graventoren you will not only find a museum, but you can enjoy a spectacular view of the Scheldt.

The 18th-century Church of Our Lady of Visitation is also worth a visit. And a little further you come across the statue of Mercator, who literally and figuratively put Rupelmonde on the map. Yes, here you are literally a tourist in your own municipality!

Unwind completely in the green environment

Thanks to the exceptional location of residence De Vliet on the Scheldedijk, you can walk or cycle into nature in no time. Along the way you stop in charming old fishing villages to enjoy fresh eel or a fresh Duvel.

The Rupelmondse Kreek is literally behind your corner and is the start for a journey of discovery in the polders of Kruibeke. Keep your eyes peeled for numerous beavers, roe deer, otters and meadow birds in the largest flood plain in Flanders!

In the summer you can use the water ferry after a short crossing to look at things from the other side and explore the Scheldeland further.

Make the most of the good connections

The commercial centers of Temse, Sint-Niklaas and Bornem are a stone's throw from your underground parking garage. For an extensive shopping trip, you can reach the Waasland Shopping Center in fifteen minutes.

Even if you have to look a little further, the strategic location in the Waasland comes in handy. The N16, E17 and A12 connect you in no time with Antwerp, Ghent and Brussels for work and leisure.

Not in a hurry? Via the polders you cycle through nature past Kruibeke and Burcht all the way to the center of Antwerp.

Kies zelf je Afwerking

De standaard afwerking is reeds van een hoog niveau om het luxegevoel te waarborgen. Met de keuzebudgetten geef je jouw appartement een persoonlijke touch. Uiteraard staat het je vrij om nog verder te upgraden, hierbij staat onze interieurarchitect je graag bij.

Koken in stijl in jouw keuken

Met keuze uit meer dan 15 kleuren voor de binnenkant en de fronten van het keukenmeubilair, en 12 beschikbare kleuren voor het werkblad, kook je in (jouw) stijl. De keukeninrichting wordt apart ontworpen naar jouw persoonlijke smaak.

Standaard worden toestellen voorzien van Smeg (of gelijkwaardig) aangeboden, maar je kiest dit vrij bij de vastgestelde leverancier.

Tegels, laminaat of parket kies je zelf!

De leefruimte, keuken, inkom, berging, toilet en badkamer worden standaard opgeleverd met een keramische tegel die je zelf vrij kiest bij onze leverancier. Ook de wandbetegeling hou je in de hand voor de keuken en de badkamer.

De slaapkamer en nachthal krijgen een gezellige sfeer met een laminaatvloer. Uiteraard kan je verder upgraden naar parket of grotere tegelformaten indien je dit wenst.
